The Task Team

No task is too large or too small. We are The Task Team, LLC, and we specialize in remodeling (any size project). We also offer lawn care, and full service moving and hauling. Since 2018, the great people of the Pacific NW have entrusted The Task Team, LLC, to complete their home improvement projects. We look forward to earning your trust and business as well.
